Virtual World: Black Hole Space Station

Blocky Animal Added: Wall - E from assignment 2

Hello traveller! This world is a space station surrounded by
black holes on all sides.
About this world (or Game):
To escape the black hole, you will have to find a few things/materials.
- This space station has 3 pyramids (yellow top, blue top, and
textured top). Use the keys A and D to go left
and right to explore all pyramids. Every pyramid has
some material (gold, silver, or energy: represented by a neon cube)
hidden inside it.
- If you want to get that material, go near the pyramid, and try to
remove the blocks. Blocks can be removed by clicking on the
"Delete Block" button. Remove them, and get the material!
- You can build add your own blocks too by clicking on the "Add Block Textured"
button. If you click that, a block will be added in front of you.
To delete it, you can press the "Delete Block" button.
- You also have your personal robot assistant: Mr. Wall-E. You can
control Wall-E by the sliders below the virtual world. Move ahead in this
world (by pressing W) to go close to Wall-E.
- If you want WALL-E to wave a hello: press on the "Animation On" button.
To stop the annimation, press on the "Animation Off" button.
- Press shift to get a view of the whole world and for Wall-E to do something funky.
- To move around, use the following keys: A, S, D, W, Q, and E.
You can also use your mouse to turn left and right.
- Also, as a traveller, you must know that in this world, all the letters
have lateral inversion and we justify it by the curvature of
space time as we are in the middle of 4 black holes.
- Another feature of this futuristic world: There are no collisions. If you
keep walking, you might as well go inside a block. The general laws of
physics seem to be broken here.


Fun fact: Normally if you add a block, you will add a textured block. But if you
add it on top of a colored block such as the magenta block on the boundry, you will
get a colored block! All the best for your journey!
