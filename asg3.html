Virtual World: Black Hole Space Station

Hello traveller! This world is a space station surrounded by
black holes on all sides.
To escape the black hole, you will have to find a few things/materials.
This space station has 3 pyramids (use the keys A and D to go left
and right to explore all pyramids.) Every pyramid has
some material (gold, silver, energy: represented by a neon cube).
If you want to get that material,
go near the pyramid, and try to remove the blocks
(delete blocks by pressing on Delete block key)
and extract the material. For deleting the block,
you will have to stand at the correct position in front
of the block.


You can also add your own blocks by clicking
on the Add Textured block button. You can again delete them,
by clicking on the Delete Block button.

You also have your personal robot assistant: Mr. Wall-E. Also, all letters
here in are inverted and we reason it as a result of the curvature of space time.
You can use a number of keys to control Wall E.

All the best for your journey!
Beaware of the Magenta boundaries!
